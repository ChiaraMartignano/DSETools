DSETools

Welcome to DSETools,
the Catalogue of Software Tools for Digital Scholarly Editing!

What started as an appendix to my PhD Thesis and was later published on Zenodo as an Excel spreadsheet has now become a digital resource in its own right. Given how precious and difficult the information in this catalogue is to retrieve, I hope it helps my fellow researchers to find useful tools with which to implement more efficient digital editorial workflows.

The Catalogue is currently under active development. For this reason, the information may still be incomplete or uncorrect. As soon as the work is complete, I will release a new official version of the catalogue on Zenodo. Please help me to perfect and grow this resource by providing your feedback as illustrated below.
